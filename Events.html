Janaranjani Varnam
By Dr.Padma Govardhan


Dr.Padma Govardhan (Venkata Padma Chitti) has done extensive research on Sankarabharanam and its Janya raagas.

Her thesis was submitted and approved at the Kodaikanal Univeristy,Chennai in the year 2014
after more than five year long research and exploration. Her work is reviewed and studied by many today.


Janaranjani Varnam by Tiger K.Varadaacharyulu was one of the many gems that Dr.Padma Govardhan has given to the world of Carnatic Music.
