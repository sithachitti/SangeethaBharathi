Raga Priya Margazhi 2020 Performance
1. ituvaNTi samayana, saranga, khanda chapu, Vina Kuppayyar
2. nannu brOvAnika (venkatESa pancaratnam), mukhAri, Adi, Vina Kuppayyar
Vina Kuppayyar was a distinguished disciple of Tyagaraja who lived from c1798 to c1904. Although famous for his varnams (in begada, bilahari, etc), he has composed many krithis as well as divyanAma kirtanams on Lord Krishna. The Venkatesa pancharatna are sometimes wrongly attributed to Saint Thyagaraja. The five compistions on Lord Venkateswara by Vina Kuppayyar are:
  • nIvE gatiyani-darbAr-ADi
  • bAgumIraganu nAtO-shankarAbharaNa-rUpaka
  • mammu brOcu-simhEndramadhyam-Adi
  • nannu brOvanika-mukhAri-Adi
  • sarOjAkSuni-sAvEri-rUpaka
The composition in mukhari has poetic beauty and features muktayi swaram after anupallavi and a madhyamakala passage after charanam.
