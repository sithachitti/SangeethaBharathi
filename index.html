Carrying forward a legacy...


We at Sangeetha Bharathi belong to Thyagaraja's shishya parampara and continue to uphold this tradition of Music for Bhakthi and Salvation and teach our students that Music is divine and has to be learnt with Bhakthi and dedication.




 

Spreading Our Wings


Sangeetha Bharathi started as an at home institute by Dr Padma Mallela almost 20 years back at Auckland, Newzealand. Over the years, it grew like a river with a prestige that students live music when they learn at Sangeetha Bharathi.
